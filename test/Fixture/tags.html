Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“This will be our first game released on steam and we want to fully prepare for it. We, a group of college students, want to make high quality game; therefore, we take priority on playerâs feeling and comments. We hope that in Early Access we can get useful feedback from community, so that this work really involved us all together.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“About one month.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“This is a so-called complete version. As we are now still in part-time development, the full version is supposed to be more attractive in art effect and also the narrative content will be improved.
We will add more extra gameplay mechanics in some stages so that the whole process will be much more interesting. The feedback from community will definitely give us new ideas that we can use for
enjoyable game experience.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current early version is fully playable and players can get to know the core game play and the full story line. The whole gameplay lasts for 6-10 hours. There are 11 stages from easy to hard. All sets of gameplay elements are designed on the magic cube. Including AI enemies, traps, elements of narrative design and collectible items. What we are mostly proud of is the set of puzzlesâhow we make up every stage with those elements.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Price may not change when we leave early access.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We take community as the key role in our dev process. When we put this game on Greenlight, we have received a lot of great ideas from community, and these were all based on the only trailer we released. We know that community is more important than we thought.

We will first invite players from different channels to steam page. Weâll collect all suggestion, ideas and other issues. These will be listed and discussed in our group and then we will set plans to solve/improve issues one by one. We will keep contact with players in discussions on how we can improve and keep them updated on our future plans.

Recently we have taken our game to some famous game exhibitions and it gained quite a lot of focus. We decide to sign in SNS media channels for our game/studio and invite people in game groups to test it.

We highly appreciate all comments and feedback from players, whether negative or positive.

Twitter: @Brainagi”
