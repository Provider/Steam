About This Game

In this 2D shooter, you play as the streamer Matias Candia, find those evil hackers who stole your channel and kidnapped The Chat! Fighting against different types of enemies, rescuing your friends, and... Off-course, drinking Bepis!

In Matias Candia: LORD DOOMER, travel around the world, rescuing your mates and sidekicks. All while you stream it in the fastest time possible on the Dark Web, earning cryptocurrencies so you can buy any kind of weapon and clothes.

Aim with your mouse and shoot anyone who gets in your path to recover what's yours!

Features:


  • Singleplayer
  • Story-mode.
  • Infinite-mode. (Unlockable)
  • Character and weapon customization.

(The game is still on its alpha version, some desings may be subject to modifications.)

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 7
    • Processor: Intel Pentium
    • Memory: 2 GB RAM
    • Graphics: GeForce 8600 GT
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Storage: 256 MB available space
    • Additional Notes: These requirements are not final.
    Recommended:
    • OS: Windows 10
    • Processor: Intel Core i3
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM
    • Graphics: GeForce GT 530
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Storage: 256 MB available space
    • Additional Notes: These requirements are not final.
